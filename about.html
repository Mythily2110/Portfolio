About Me

Who I Am

I am a Data Engineer with 3+ years of experience building cloud-native data pipelines and AI-enabled systems across AWS and Azure, currently pursuing a Master's in Artificial Intelligence & Business Analytics at the University of South Florida.

My work spans ETL workflows, SQL data modeling, NLP/ML pipeline support, and production deployments using FastAPI, Docker, and Streamlit. I bridge technical implementation with business outcomes — from raw data ingestion to executive dashboards.

Beyond engineering, I serve as a Research Assistant at USF's Behavioral AI Lab, studying human-AI interaction, trust, and emotional impact of AI systems. I'm also actively building an AI-powered mental health research platform that uses a 10-model behavioral analysis framework to surface anonymized insights from user activity.
